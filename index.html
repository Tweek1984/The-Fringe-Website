About Us

A unisex salon, family run business owned by Lisa Lewis; a very experienced hairdresser in all aspects of hairdressing.

The Fringe hairstylists specialise in cutting, colouring and styling people's hair in a way that complements their features,

such as skin complexion and face shape in order to enhance their overall appearance.

However, there is so much more to being a hairdresser than just cutting or styling hair.
